Engine Enum

TextXAlignment

---

Determines horizontal alignment of text.

Used by [TextLabel](/docs/reference/engine/classes/TextLabel#TextXAlignment),
[TextButton](/docs/reference/engine/classes/TextButton#TextXAlignment), and
[TextBox](/docs/reference/engine/classes/TextBox#TextXAlignment).

Items

| Name | Value | Summary |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Left | 0 | Alignment starts at the left side of the GUI object. |
| Right | 1 | Alignment starts at the right side of the GUI object. |
| Center | 2 | Aligned starts at the center of the GUI object and spreads equally left and right. |
