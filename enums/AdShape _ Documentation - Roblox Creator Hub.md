AdShape

---

Describes the form factor of an ad in an [AdGui](/docs/reference/engine/classes/AdGui). There is currently
only one available size.

Items

| Name | Value | Summary |
| --- | --- | --- |
| HorizontalRectangle | 1 | The ad has an aspect ratio of 16:9 and a minimum size of 8 by 4.5 studs. |
