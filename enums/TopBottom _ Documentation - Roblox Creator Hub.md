Engine Enum

TopBottom

---

Sets where the object is on the side of its parent.

Items

| Name | Value | Summary |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Top | 0 | The object is above its parent. |
| Center | 1 | The object is centered around the center of its parent. |
| Bottom | 2 | The object is below its parent. |
