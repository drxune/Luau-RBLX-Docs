Engine Enum

TextYAlignment

---

Determines vertical alignment of text.

Used by [TextLabel](/docs/reference/engine/classes/TextLabel#TextYAlignment),
[TextButton](/docs/reference/engine/classes/TextButton#TextYAlignment), and
[TextBox](/docs/reference/engine/classes/TextBox#TextYAlignment).

Items

| Name | Value | Summary |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Top | 0 | Alignment starts at the top of the GUI object. |
| Center | 1 | Aligned starts at the center of the GUI object and spreads equally toward the top and bottom. |
| Bottom | 2 | Alignment starts at the bottom of the GUI object. |
