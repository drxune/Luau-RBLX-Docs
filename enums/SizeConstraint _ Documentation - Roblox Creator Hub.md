Engine Enum

SizeConstraint

---

Used to constrain the scale of a GUI object.

Items

| Name | Value | Summary |
| --- | --- | --- |
| RelativeXY | 0 | The scale of X and Y are independent. |
| RelativeXX | 1 | The scale of X is shared with Y. |
| RelativeYY | 2 | The scale of Y is shared with X. |
