[UIPadding](/docs/reference/engine/classes/UIPadding) applies padding to the borders of the parent
[GuiObject](/docs/reference/engine/classes/GuiObject), either as a pixel amount ([Offset](/docs/reference/engine/datatypes/UDim#Offset)) or
percentage of the parent's size ([Scale](/docs/reference/engine/datatypes/UDim#Scale)).
